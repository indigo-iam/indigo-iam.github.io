IAM v1.10.2

The v1.10.2 release includes bug fixes
By IAM development team | Friday, September 27, 2024

The IAM development team is pleased to announce the release of INDIGO IAM v1.10.2.

This release mainly provides bug fixes and enhancements, as can be seen in the release notes.

The major highlight includes a stronger password quality check, which needs to meet the following criteria:

  • at least 8 characters long
  • include at least one uppercase letter
  • include at least one lowercase letter
  • include at least one number
  • include at least one special character (e.g., @$!%*?&).

As usual, the IAM docker image can be obtained from DockerHub.
